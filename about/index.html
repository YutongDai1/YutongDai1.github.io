About me

Future Software Engineer

Hi there! I'm Alex, an aspiring software engineer with a passion for creating innovative solutions that make a difference. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, I'm on a journey to explore the vast world of technology and harness its power to solve real-world problems. From developing small utility programs to contributing to open-source projects, every line of code I write brings me closer to understanding the impact of technology on our lives. My academic projects and internships have introduced me to a variety of programming languages and frameworks, with a particular interest in Python, Java, and web development. I believe in continuous learning and am always on the lookout for new challenges that push the boundaries of my knowledge and skills.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me immersed in a variety of activities that fuel my creativity and curiosity. I'm an avid reader, with a soft spot for science fiction and fantasy novels.
